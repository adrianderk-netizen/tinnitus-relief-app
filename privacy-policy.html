Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 23, 2026

Paulabac, LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Tinnitus Relief Pro mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally: Tinnitus Relief Pro stores your settings, therapy sessions, and preferences locally on your device using HTML5 Local Storage. This includes:

  • Matched tinnitus frequencies
  • Audio therapy settings (volume, waveform, notch parameters)
  • Session history and progress statistics
  • User profiles and preferences

Information We May Collect:

  • Subscription Information: When you purchase a subscription through Apple's App Store, your payment information is processed by Apple. We receive confirmation of your subscription status through RevenueCat but do not store payment details.
  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and app version to provide technical support.
  • Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage data to improve the app (e.g., which features are used most frequently).

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Process and manage your subscription
  • Improve app features and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Send important updates about the App

Data Storage and Security

Your therapy data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and in-app purchases. Apple's Privacy Policy applies.
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management. RevenueCat's Privacy Policy applies.

Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored locally on your device
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data
  • Opt-out of analytics (if implemented)
  • Request information about data we may have collected

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Medical Disclaimer

This App is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, or cure any medical condition. The information provided through this App is for informational and therapeutic support purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
