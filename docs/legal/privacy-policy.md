# Privacy Policy

**Tinnitussaurus**

*Last updated: February 17, 2026*

## Information We Collect

Tinnitussaurus stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

### Data Stored on Your Device

- **Therapy session history** (duration, frequency settings, timestamps)
- **Journal entries** you create
- **App preferences** (volume, reminder settings, color preferences)
- **Tinnitus frequency profiles** you configure

### Data We Do Not Collect

- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Contacts or address book data
- Microphone or camera data
- Analytics or usage tracking data

## Third-Party Services

### Apple StoreKit

If you purchase a premium subscription, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment information. See [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/) for details.

## Data Retention

All app data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. You can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the app.

## Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/adrianderk-netizen/tinnitus-relief-app).
